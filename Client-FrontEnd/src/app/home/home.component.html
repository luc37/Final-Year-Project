Renegade Survivor

This is a MUD that attempts to give greater cross room interaction. Characters must scour the world, hunt for the overlord and defeat him. Beware his bandits, they are hunting your every step!

Face Direction

Every character has a direction they face so theycan be snuck up by other players.

Sight Detection

Characters can see other characters in other rooms if there are no walls between them and see what actions they undertake.

Smell Detection

Characters emit detectable smells after eating food.

Sound Detection

Characters can hear the actions of other characters in they are loud and close enough.

Sense detection impacted by the world

feature details

Bandits Hunt by sound

Bandits uses sounds to detect characters and hunt them down

Objects to hide in

Characters can hide in, under and behind objects in the world to evade detection.

Interactive Lights

Rooms are connected to circuits that control lights. Find light switches to turn on or off the lights on all rooms on the circuit.

Actions take time

All actions take time to execute making every action count.

Room impacting commands

Find the bomb and detonate to cause an explosion. It literally blows up rooms destroying objects and walls.
